About the Logo

My inspiration for the logo comes from the company wanting the parrot to be the mascot. I wanted the logo to have high energy and be appealing to all ages. Children will like the logo because it is cartoonish and parents/adults wont care about the logo because they’ll love the chocolate!

The logo I created is pretty straight forward. It is a parrot who is made up of the the company’s colours, red, blue, and gold, and there is a piece of chocolate in the birds mouth.

My logo is effective because it is very straight forward. There is a picture of the company’s name, Parrot, and there parrot is carrying chocolate in it’s beak. If the customer remembers the logo, they will remember the brand and what it is that they’re selling.

The logo I made uses the companies colours, which makes the logo very colourful and vibrant. It is also an animal. Most of the competitors don’t have pictures as their logo, they have words.
